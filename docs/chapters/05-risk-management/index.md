# Chapter 5: Risk Management

## Summary

Before committing to a design direction, engineers must evaluate whether their solution is safe and identify what could go wrong. This chapter teaches students to apply safety standards to their designs, systematically identify risks, analyze their likelihood and impact, and develop strategies to reduce or eliminate them — a critical step before the project proposal is written.
