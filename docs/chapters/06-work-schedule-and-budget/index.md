# Chapter 6: Project Work Schedule and Budget

## Summary

With a problem defined and risks assessed, students now plan the execution of their practicum project in detail. This chapter covers the full project planning toolkit — breaking work into manageable pieces, building a realistic schedule with industry-standard tools, and developing a budget grounded in real cost research — so students can demonstrate to stakeholders that their solution is achievable within the course timeframe.
