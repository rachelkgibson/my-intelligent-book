# Chapter 2: Engineering Foundations and Industry Examination

## Summary

This chapter builds the conceptual foundation for the entire course by contrasting the engineering design process with the scientific method, and introducing systems thinking as a lens for understanding how engineering organizations operate. Students apply these ideas directly through the Engineering Industry Scavenger Hunt and a structured comparison activity.

## Assignments

- **Engineering Industry Scavenger Hunt** — CTE-23, CTE-24, CTE-25, CTE-26, CTE-27, CTE-28, CTE-29, CTE-30
- **Engineering Design Process vs Scientific Method Comparison** — CTE-45

## Concepts Covered

This chapter covers the following 8 concepts from the learning graph:

1. Scientific Method
2. Hypothesis Testing
3. Engineering Design Process
4. Engineering vs Science
5. Systems Thinking
6. System Inputs and Outputs
7. Feedback Loops
8. Subsystem Interactions
