Chapter 8: Project Execution, Checkpoints, and Final Presentation

Summary

This chapter covers the full project execution phase — from building and testing a prototype, through modeling and simulation checkpoints, to documenting progress in a technical report and delivering a final presentation to stakeholders. Students apply systems thinking to their completed solution and demonstrate the technical and professional readiness they have developed across the entire course.

Assignments

  • Weekly Work Log — CTE-57, CTE-66, CTE-60
  • Checkpoint 1: Design Model Review — CTE-61
  • Checkpoint 2: Project Progress Presentation — CTE-66, CTE-57
  • Final Report and Project Presentation — CTE-63, CTE-64, CTE-66, CTE-60, CTE-50
